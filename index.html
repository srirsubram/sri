Sriram Subramanian

66-72 Gower Street · London , WC1E 6EA, · UK · s.subramanian [at] ucl.ac.uk

I am Professor of Computer Science at UCL where I currently hold a Royal Academy of Engineering chair in Emerging Technologies. My primary research interest is in the area of human-computer interaction. I am particularly interested in creating novel user-interfaces through the computational manipulation of acoustic wavefronts.

I am always looking for excellent students and research fellows to join my team. Please take a look at Openings.
